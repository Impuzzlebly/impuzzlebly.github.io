Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my progress?

Game progress is saved locally on your device. If you uninstall the game or switch devices, progress may be lost.

How do I remove ads?

Some of our games offer an ad-free option through an in-app purchase. Check the settings menu within the game for available options.

The game is crashing or not loading

Try these steps:

  • Make sure your device has the latest OS updates
  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the game

If the issue persists, please contact us with your device model and OS version.

I have a suggestion or feedback

We love hearing from you! Send your ideas to our support email and we'll take them into consideration for future updates.
