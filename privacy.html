Our games use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads

Our games are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through a third-party service, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data directly, we do not retain any personal information. Third-party services may retain data according to their own privacy policies.
